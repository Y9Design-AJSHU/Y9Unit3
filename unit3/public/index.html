Welcome to Viralert Test Request. If you are at a high risk of developing a serious illness related to COVID-19, and have symptomse related to COVID-19, please enter your symptoms and request a test by submitting the form below. If we have deemed that you are likely to have COVID-19, within 5 days, a test will be delivered to your door (we will communicate with you by email throughout this whole process).

Viralert is a service that allows those who are at a high risk of getting a serious illness from COVID-19 to receive tests as soon as possible. Volunteers will drive the tests to you and back to the lab, and will contact you with your results. Because not everyone can afford tests, we ask that if you can, would you please donate to allow us to buy these tests. Thanks!
